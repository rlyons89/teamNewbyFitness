Newby's Boxing Method

Wednesdays 6am – 7am


1726 Kalorama Rd NW, Washington, DC 20009


Newby's Boxing Method combines basic boxing fundamentals, plyometrics and high intensity interval (HIT) training to boost your heart rate and burn hundreds of calories. The class will encompass the technical aspects of boxing with core strengthening exercises. I am a high energy trainer, and while we will work hard, we will most definitely have fun. All fitness levels are welcome and encouraged to join.
